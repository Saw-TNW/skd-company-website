Our Mission

"SKD Auto Transport is a trusted provider of hauler truck services and auto repair solutions, dedicated to keeping vehicles — and communities — moving forward. We specialize in safe, efficient vehicle transport and reliable repair work, serving individuals, businesses, and organizations with professionalism and care.

Beyond our services, we believe in making a difference where it matters most — at home. By hiring and training local talent, we create meaningful job opportunities that strengthen our community and empower people to build better futures. At SKD Auto Transport, we’re not just about moving vehicles; we’re about driving progress."
